About Us

Earlier company was established in 1987 in the name of SUSI ENGINEERING INDUSTRIESS. Later in 2004 the name has been changed to SUSI INDUSTRIESS. Having experience of 30 years in the Design and manufacture of Engineering Machines. In the field of Engineering Machines SUSI INDUSTRIESS is leading manufactures of paper cone and paper tube making machines.

Precise & Powerful Design

The highly developed state-of-the-art infrastructural facility at SUSI Industriess, has been parted into several highly operational units, for reasons of attaining smoother and effective management of the firm’s operations. The facility laced with all the necessary machinery and equipment, helps us in attaining a number of the firm’s predefined goals and targets. To maximize the firm’s reach in the market, the facility undergoes regular up-gradation. In addition to this, the abidance of industry ethics, have helped in generating a huge client base.

Continues Research & Development

We believe that our continuous research and development is what allows us to establish efficient and environmentally friendly business practices and to remain competitive in an ever changing market. Monitoring feedback at times allows for strategic decision making, benchmarks can be established and compared month to month or quarter to quarter.

Premium Support

Support gives us the value of our company and our dedication towards the business. An Engineer will be assigned for every support & calls. Our support doesn’t end within the product delivery, it leads the end of customer satisfaction.

Proven History

With a proven history of success in manufacture of Engineering Machines, SUSI Industriess is confident the machines supplied to clients are the absolute best available. SUSI Industriess embraces your sourcing goals and works tirelessly to establish long-term supplier relationships.
